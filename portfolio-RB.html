HTML, Bootstrap, SCSS, Javascript and plugins

This is a website project for a company that are looking to expand there online presence and orders. The brief was the colors had to match their logo, they wanted to take orders online but online payment on receipt of invoice and only pick up. They are also a distributor of 3rd party products but wanted (and were authorised) to have them coded direct into the website. Although still under construction the frontend and most of the backend is finished the product coding is taking my time - i have learnt e-commerce platforms can be quite time consuming even when using a CMS. I am also moving images across to SVG as these help the webpage to load faster and help with performance. My goal is to ensure that the Web site will be above 90 on all Lighthouse report tests. I have also added a few other features to the website to help with the sales process. The website is currently under construction and will be available soon.
